Shipping Information

We ship premium gym equipment across Pakistan with reliable delivery partners. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping services.

Delivery Coverage

We deliver to all major cities and regions in Pakistan:

  • Major Cities: Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta
  • Other Cities: Sialkot, Gujranwala, Bahawalpur, Sargodha, Sukkur, and more
  • Remote Areas: Available with extended delivery time (contact for quote)

Shipping Rates

Free Shipping: Orders over Rs. 50,000 qualify for FREE delivery to major cities!

Standard shipping rates:

  • Small Items (Accessories, Bands, Gloves): Rs. 200 - 350
  • Medium Items (Dumbbells, Kettlebells, Benches): Rs. 500 - 1,000
  • Large Items (Treadmills, Multi-gyms, Racks): Rs. 1,500 - 3,500
  • Remote Areas: Additional Rs. 500 - 1,500

Delivery Timeline

  • Major Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 7-14 business days
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days (additional charges apply)
Note: Delivery times may vary during peak seasons (Eid, New Year) or due to weather conditions.

Installation Services

Professional installation available for:

  • Treadmills and cardio machines: Rs. 2,000 - 4,000
  • Multi-gym stations: Rs. 3,000 - 6,000
  • Power racks and squat racks: Rs. 1,500 - 3,000
  • Complete gym setup: Contact for custom quote

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Email confirmation with tracking number
  • SMS updates on delivery status
  • Phone call from delivery team before arrival
  • Real-time tracking via our website

Delivery Guidelines

  • Someone must be present to receive the delivery
  • Valid ID required for verification
  • Inspect items before signing delivery receipt
  • Report any damage immediately to driver and customer service
  • Heavy items delivered to ground floor only (additional fee for upper floors)

Missed Delivery? Our delivery team will attempt delivery twice. After that, items are held at the depot for 3 days before being returned to warehouse.
