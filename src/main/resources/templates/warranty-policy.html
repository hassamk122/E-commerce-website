Warranty Policy

At Fitness Depot, we stand behind the quality of our products. All gym equipment purchased from us comes with comprehensive warranty coverage to ensure your peace of mind.

Warranty Coverage

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and workmanship issues under normal use conditions. The warranty period varies by product category:

  • Cardio Equipment (Treadmills, Bikes, Ellipticals): 2 years on frame, 1 year on parts and electronics
  • Strength Training Equipment: 5 years on frame, 2 years on moving parts
  • Free Weights & Dumbbells: Lifetime warranty on structural integrity
  • Accessories & Small Items: 6 months to 1 year depending on product
Important: Warranty is valid only with original purchase receipt and begins from the date of purchase.

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Structural frame integrity issues
  • Motor and electronic component failures (within warranty period)
  • Mechanical parts failure under normal use
  • Paint or powder coating defects

What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear including upholstery, grips, and cables
  • Damage from improper use, abuse, or neglect
  • Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Commercial use of home equipment
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality
  • Damage during transportation after delivery

Claiming Your Warranty

To file a warranty claim, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team at +92 300 1234567 or email warranty@fitnessdepot.clone
  • Provide your order number and purchase receipt
  • Describe the issue with photos or videos if possible
  • Our technical team will assess the claim within 48 hours
  • Approved claims will be repaired or replaced at our discretion

Response Time: We aim to respond to all warranty claims within 48 hours. Most repairs or replacements are completed within 7-14 business days.
