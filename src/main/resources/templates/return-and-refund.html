Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your gym equipment, we offer a hassle-free return and refund policy.

Return Window

You may return most new, unopened items within 14 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in original condition with all packaging, accessories, and documentation.

Extended Return Period: Items purchased during sale events may have extended return windows. Check your order confirmation for specific details.

Eligibility for Returns

Items eligible for return must meet the following conditions:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging intact (boxes, styrofoam, protective wrapping)
  • All accessories, manuals, and documentation included
  • No signs of assembly or installation
  • Product tags and labels attached

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or personalized equipment
  • Opened or used hygiene products (yoga mats, resistance bands, gloves)
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse
  • Equipment assembled or installed

Return Process

To initiate a return:

  • Contact our customer service within the return window
  • Provide your order number and reason for return
  • We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number
  • Pack the item securely in original packaging
  • Write the RA number clearly on the outside of the package
  • Schedule pickup or drop off at our facility

Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Inspection completed within 2-3 business days
  • Refund processed within 5-7 business days
  • Funds appear in your account within 7-10 business days
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable

Restocking Fee: A 15% restocking fee applies to opened or assembled items if return is approved on a case-by-case basis.

Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for defective or damaged items. For size or color exchanges, please contact customer service to check availability before returning your item.
